Advocacy Through PRIDE

Gender Inclusive Pronoun Awareness

The PRIDE Club (People Representing Inclusion, Diversity, and Excellence) is a student club at Century College that allows students to get involved with advocacy for the LGBTQ+ community, along with providing a space for students to meet each other. Involvement in this group allowed me to participate in volunteer work for local organizations and attend conferences across the Midwest. I was president of the club for two years and in that time I was able to advocate for greater bathroom access on campus, create community events to bring many clubs across campus together, and break down barriers for students changing their name on campus by aiding in simplifying the process.
